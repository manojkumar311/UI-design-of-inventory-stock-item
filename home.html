ABOUT US

We came into existence in 1986. In 1991, the company was formed into a legal corporation. However, it actively started working as a fully integrated pharmaceutical company in 1995. Today, we have more than 14,000 employees and are heading towards a turnover of INR 50000 million.Today, We operate in 22 overseas destinations across Asia, Africa, South-East Asia and Gulf countries. The company has begun operations in the markets of Commonwealth Independent States, Uzbekistan and Tajikistan.
With an employee base of 14000 and heading towards a turnover of INR 50000 million, we aspire to aid the community in leading a healthy life by formulating, developing, commercializing, and delivering affordable and accessible medicines that satisfy urgent medical needs.
We are among the top 4 leading pharmaceutical companies in the country and constantly strive to be the best. We provide products that range from prescriptions medicines to OTC and FMCG products. Our business model aligned to ‘making quality medicines affordable to all’ differentiates us substantially from other companies. We dream big and looks forward to expanding services from the local to global markets.
